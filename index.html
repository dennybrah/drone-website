Why Work With Me

I'm Denny, and I've been capturing Las Vegas from every angle since long before I had a drone in the air. With over 10 years in photo and video editing, I understand that great media isn't just about the shot; it's about the story you're trying to tell.

Since earning my Part 107 in 2025, I've combined technical flying skills with a decade of post-production experience to deliver polished, professional work across real estate, business marketing, and video production. I'm based in Las Vegas (since 1998) and know the valley inside and out, especially the West Side from southwest to northwest. That local knowledge means I'm not just pointing a camera; I'm highlighting what makes each location unique.

What You Can Expect:

  • 24-hour turnaround on most edited footage and photos
  • Flexible scheduling that works around your timelines
  • Responsive communication: I keep you in the loop from booking to delivery
  • A meticulous eye for detail: I'm critical of my own work so you don't have to be

I don't just meet the brief; I make sure you're genuinely happy with the final product. Whether it's a property listing, promotional content for your business, or a full production, I'll do what it takes to deliver work that stands out.
